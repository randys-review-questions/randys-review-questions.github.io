Randy's Review Questions

Marching Band

Based on content taught by the staff at the George N. Parks Drum Major Academy in Summer 2019 and the CHSMRB instructors in Fall 2019.

1. During the chasse portion of the winds dance warmup, your hands should be...

    Covering your eyes
    On top of your head
    On your hips
    Underneath your feet

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: On your hips

2. The only 100% effective way to fix a tempo issue is to...

    Always watch, never listen
    Always listen, never watch
    Stay where you are and stop moving
    Not let a tempo issue happen in the first place

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Not let a tempo issue happen in the first place

3. In cold temperatures, wind instruments tend to be...

    Self aware
    Flat
    Sharp
    Loud

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Flat

4. What instrument has a solo at letter M of "When the Clock Strikes 12?"

    Trumpet
    Mellophone
    Alto Saxophone
    Baritone Saxophone

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Baritone Saxophone

5. When a wire connecting pit (front ensemble) equipment is in front of you, and it is not practical to walk around it, one should step _____ the wire.

    over
    under
    through
    All of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: over

6. What 4/4 conducting pattern has no rebounds on beats 1 and 3, has only rebounds on beats 2 and 4, and is good for cruise control?

    Legato
    Stop Motion
    Thump
    Down-Angle

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Thump

7. All of the following are considered guard equipment EXCEPT...

    Xylophones
    Rifles
    Sabres
    Flags

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Xylophones

8. Which instrument plays the first note in the "Power Chords" exercise?

    Tubas
    Trumpets
    Alto Saxophones
    Flutes

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Tubas

9. Who is the ultimate timekeeper of the marching band?

    The Tubas
    The Battery/Drumline
    The Front Ensemble/Pit
    The Guard

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The Battery/Drumline

10. How many counts are there in the entirety of "When the Clock Strikes 12," from the tick-tocks to the end of the tag?

    987
    1025
    1361
    1447

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 1025
