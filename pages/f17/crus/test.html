1. What muscle facilitates breathing when singing?

    Quadriceps
    Biceps
    Triceps
    Diaphragm

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Diaphragm

2. Why is diction important when singing?

    So the audience can get on their phones ASAP
    So the audience can understand what the singers are saying
    So the audience would get soothed
    So the audience would fall asleep quickly

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: So the audience can understand what the singers are saying

3. When singing, what should the position of the shoulders be?

    Front and down
    Back and down
    Front and up
    Back and up

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Back and down

4. Which of these is NOT a characteristic of good tone?

    Clear
    Resonant
    Strident
    Free

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Strident

5. Good intonation is defined as...

    Singing a note too sharp
    Singing a note too flat
    Singing a note ON THE BULL'S EYE
    Singing the wrong note

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Singing a note ON THE BULL'S EYE

6. The abbreviation of a ritardando is...

    rdo.
    rito.
    radado.
    rit.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: rit.

7. A fermata means to hold a note until...

    Everyone runs out of breath
    The conductor cuts off
    Someone's head cracks open
    Someone rage-quits

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The conductor cuts off

8. When singing, your eyes should be...

    Looking at the conductor
    Looking at the audience
    Looking at your peers
    What are eyes?

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Looking at the conductor

9. What happens when you call Rosie "Ma?"

    You die
    You are locked in the closet at the back of the room for 10 years
    You are socially shunned by the rest of the class until you die
    Rosie is much disappoint

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Rosie is much disappoint

10. How wide should your feet be when standing (for singing)?

    Shoulder width
    Hip width
    Elbow width
    Together

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Shoulder width
