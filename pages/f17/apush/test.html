1. The colony of Maryland was created as a safe haven for which religion?

    Catholicism
    Presbyterianism
    Baptism
    Society of Friends
    Antinomianism

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Catholicism

2. Shays' Rebellion led many to argue that...

    The federal government was too powerful
    The federal government was corrupt
    The federal government was not strong enough
    The ordinary citizen was oppressed
    The ordinary citizen was an idiot

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The federal government was not strong enough

3. Andrew Jackson's Specie Circular said that...

    Only hard currency can be used in payments.
    No currency except paper money may be used in payments.
    The national bank should be destroyed.
    The national bank needs another term.
    Henry Clay should be barred from running for office.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Only hard currency can be used in payments.

4. The Declaration of Sentiments from the Seneca Falls Convention in 1848 had many parallels with...

    Abigail Adam's letters to John Adam's
    Susan Beach Anthony's speech
    The Declaraion of Independence
    Susan B. Anthony's speech
    The Feminine Mystique by Betty Friedan

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The Declaraion of Independence

5. During the Election of 1864, Lincoln ran as part of the _____ party.

    Federalist
    Democrat
    Republican
    Union
    Free Soil

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Union

6. In the Compromise of 1877, as a concession to giving Rutherford B. Hayes the presidency...

    The KKK was legalized
    Military reconstruction ended
    Congressional reconstruction was passed
    Carpetbaggers were granted power over the south
    Samuel J. Tilden was chosen for vice president

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Military reconstruction ended

7. The Dawes Severalty Act of 1887 was an attempt at ______ the Native Americans.

    Pushing
    Assimilating
    Terminating
    Surrounding
    Preserving

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Assimilating

8. Muckrakers generally...

    Made intended efforts to destroy big businesses
    Sensationized stories to gain attention
    Robbed houses
    Made alcohol in their homes in the wake of prohibition
    Exposed truths in the corruption of society

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Exposed truths in the corruption of society

9. What sparked American unity during World War II?

    Hitler's invasion of Poland
    Lend Lease
    Article X
    Bombing of Hiroshima & Nagasaki
    Attack on Pearl Harbor

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Attack on Pearl Harbor

10. Lyndon B. Johnson's "War on Poverty" was dubbed as the...

    Great Depression
    New Deal
    Fair Deal
    Great Society
    Square Deal

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Great Society
