Randy's Review Questions

Marching Band

Based on content taught by the CHSMRB instructors in Fall 2017.

1. When marching forwards (in a roll-step), which part of your foot should contact the ground first?

    Heel
    Ankle
    Toe
    Platform

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Heel

2. Unless stated otherwise, during any time of marching band performance, where should your horns be pointed toward?

    The Drum Major
    The Sideline
    The Box
    The Ground

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The Box

3. During any type of 90 degree slide (for marching), what angle should your hip be at?

    30 degrees
    45 degrees
    60 degrees
    90 degrees

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 45 degrees

4. The best approach to forward marching would be to...

    Reach forward with your foot
    Lift your foot up and then plop it down
    Jump
    Push off the platform of the back foot

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Push off the platform of the back foot

5. The title of the 2017 CHSMRB Show is...

    Duality
    Midas Touch
    Transcend
    You've Changed

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Transcend
