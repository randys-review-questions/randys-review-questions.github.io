1. Spending more money than you have is often referred to as...

    Delayed Gratification
    Deficit Spending
    Capital Gain
    Capital Loss

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Deficit Spending

2. Which of the following is NOT necessary for a SMART Goal?

    Goal is realistic
    Goal is specific
    Goal has a deadline
    Goal is exceeded

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Goal is exceeded

3. When deciding between two job options, what should play a role in your decision besides the salary?

    Free Toys
    Job Benefits
    Investments
    Leisure Time

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Job Benefits

4. Medicare counts as what percentage taken off the gross payment?

    1.45%
    6.20%
    10%
    20%

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 1.45%

5. What is common in all situations of secured debt?

    Credit Card
    Collateral
    Death Penalties
    The FBI

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Collateral

6. During what hours of the day is the NYSE open in pacific time?

    6:30am-1:00pm
    7:30am-2:00pm
    8:30am-3:00pm
    9:30am-4:00pm

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 6:30am-1:00pm

7. Generally, you only want to invest in stocks when...

    You have at least a $100K house
    You have an adequate emergency fund
    Your fixed expenses are very minimal
    Your boss tells you to

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: You have an adequate emergency fund

8. Blue chip stock involves...

    Rich items
    Need items
    Toys
    Ocean gear

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Need items

9. If you make a mistake on your check and don't want ANYONE to use it, what word do you write?

    DON'T USE!
    DEATH
    STOCK
    VOID

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: VOID

10. Fixed expenses involve...

    Set payments
    Approximate payments
    Periodic payments
    No payments

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Set payments
