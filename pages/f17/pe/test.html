Randy's Review Questions

Physical Education

Based on content taught by Sarah Mik in Fall 2017 and Dan Lee in Spring 2018.

1. The magnetic needle on a compass always points...

    North
    South
    East
    West

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: North

2. During the push up test (fitness test), each push up should be about...

    10 degrees
    45 degrees
    90 degrees
    120 degrees

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 90 degrees

3. Which of the following would be a violation of the rules of swatball?

    Blocking an attempted score
    Holding the ball and running
    Hitting the ball
    Swatting the ball towards the goal

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Holding the ball and running

4. The "double bounce rule" in pickleball refers to the fact that...

    After the serve, the ball must bounce twice (once on either side) before it can be volleyed.
    After the ball bounces twice, it is considered a fault.
    As long as the ball bounces twice on the net, it doesn't have to go over the net to score.
    A ball can bounce on the heads of two people before it can no longer bounce on the head of a person.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: After the serve, the ball must bounce twice (once on either side) before it can be volleyed.

5. How many players are on a team in handball?

    4
    7
    11
    15

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 7

6. Rally scoring (in volleyball) means that...

    Only the serving team may score
    Only the receiving team may score
    No one is ever allowed to score
    Someone scores every round

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Someone scores every round

7. When catching a ball in lacrosse, it is important to...

    Have the catcher higher up than where you think the ball is going to go
    Have the catcher lower than where you think the ball is going to go
    Push the stick forward when catching
    Pull the stick back when catching

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Pull the stick back when catching

8. A score of one below par in golf is called a...

    Double Bogey
    Bogey
    Birdie
    Eagle

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Birdie

9. In tennis, when both teams are tied at 40-40, it is called...

    Game
    Deuce
    Advantage In
    Advantage Out

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Deuce

10. In PE class, the grade you get if you show up prepared is...

    85%
    94%
    97%
    100%

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 94%
