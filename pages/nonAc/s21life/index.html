Randy's Review Questions

Tufts Campus Life in Spring 2021

Based on general facts about living on the Tufts campus in Spring 2021. Contains questions about Tufts Dining, Academic Life, Campus Geography, and COVID Policies.

1. You want to use ONLY 1 lunch meal swipe. The total cost of the items in your cart should not exceed...

    $6.85
    $11.25
    $11.35
    $13.25

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: $11.35

2. You're out of meal swipes and are feeling especially narcissistic. Where can you easily steal someone else's order?

    Hotung Cafe
    Pax et Lox
    Kindlevan Cafe
    Mugar Cafe

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Mugar Cafe

3. Which of the following is/are (a) sushi option(s) served at Tufts? Select all that apply.

    California Roll
    Jumbo Roll
    Vegetable Roll
    Tuna and Salmon Combo Roll
    None of these

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answers were: California Roll, Jumbo Roll, Vegetable Roll, Tuna and Salmon Combo Roll

4. You vaguely remember that there is a Cafe inside Tisch Library that hasn't been open for almost an entire year at this point. What was it called again?

    Tower Cafe
    Stacy's Cafe
    Starbucks Coffee
    The Sink

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Tower Cafe

5. You've heard decent things about breakfast food from Commons. When is it open for breakfast?

    Weekdays 8am-10:30am
    Weekdays 9am-11am
    Weekends 10am-11am
    Every Day 10am-11am

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Weekends 10am-11am

6. What is the platform where most virtual Tufts classes are held?

    Zoom
    Microsoft Teams
    Twitch
    Google Meet

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Zoom

7. An exception to the residency requirement was established for students who studied at Tufts in Fall of 2020. Students who completed the Fall 2020 semester at Tufts now only have to complete _____ full time semesters to be eligible for graduation.

    four
    five
    six
    seven

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: six

8. A grade of an A+ corresponds to what GPA?

    4.333
    4.33
    4.3
    4

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 4

9. Which of the following is/are (an) exam time(s) according to the Tufts Exam Block schedule? Select all that apply.

    8:30 AM - 10:30 AM
    12:30 PM - 2:30 PM
    3:30 PM - 5:30 PM
    7:30 PM - 9:30 PM
    None of these

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answers were: 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM, 3:30 PM - 5:30 PM

10. All of the following are dates this semester that we did not have classes EXCEPT...

    15-Feb
    14-Mar
    2-Apr
    19-Apr

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 2-Apr

11. Which of the following is located the closest to the Gantcher Center?

    Anderson Hall
    Dowling Hall
    Halligan Hall
    Metcalf Hall

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Halligan Hall

12. The Pearson Chemistry building is located _____; Bromfield-Pearson is located _____.

    uphill, uphill
    uphill, downhill
    downhill, uphill
    downhill, downhill

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: downhill, downhill

13. Each bike that is part of the Tufts Bike Share is stored in one of two bike racks. One of them is outside Tisch Library. Where is the other one?

    Outside the SEC (Science & Engineering Complex)
    Outside the Mayer Campus Center
    Outside the track
    Outside Dowling Hall

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Outside the Mayer Campus Center

14. Select all of the following that are true statements.

    The Jumbo statue is east of the cannon.
    West Hall is east of East Hall.
    None of these

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answers were: None of these

    The Bookstore
    Dewick-Macphie Dining Hall

16. The COVID-19 test used at Tufts involves swabbing the inside of one's...

    nose.
    mouth.
    ears.
    armpits.

17. The Mods are temporary buildings built over...

    the basketball courts.
    the tennis courts.
    the baseball field.
    the track & football field.

Correct!

18. If one is in need of more masks and/or sanitizing wipes during the semester, where is one to go to pick it up?

    The Mailroom
    Gantcher Center
    Aldekman Arts Center
    Mayer Campus Center

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Mayer Campus Center

19. Wearing a face mask is required in which of the following scenarios? Select all that apply.

    When actively eating or drinking
    When running around campus
    When in one's own room with only their roommate(s)
    When brushing one's teeth
    None of these

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answers were: When running around campus

20. If you test positive for COVID-19, the period of isolation is...

    14 days
