Randy's Review Questions

CS 4: Teaching Computer Science

Based on content taught by Sam Berman and Megan Monroe in Fall 2021.

1. You encounter a student in OH (Office Hours) at 11pm, immediately before the due date of an assignment, and they say they've been exclusively working on this assignment in Halligan since 9am. It is clear based on your encounter that they are thoroughly fatigued and not thinking straight. What is the best course of action?

    Tell them to power through
    Finish the assignment for them
    Send them home and have them use a late token
    Tell them that their brain was not meant for CS

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Send them home and have them use a late token

2. All of the following would be a violation of TA personal boundaries EXCEPT...

    Asking a TA for their phone number during OH
    Approaching a TA in the dining hall and asking for programming help
    Emailing or making a Piazza post to a TA about switching lab sections for the day outside of lab time
    All of these are personal boundaries violations.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Emailing or making a Piazza post to a TA about switching lab sections for the day outside of lab time

3. When TAing during OH, if a student asks a question that you don't know the answer to, what is the best way to respond?

    Saying "I don't know" and encouraging them to post on the class forum
    Guessing what the correct answer might be
    Apologize and code something for them as compensation
    Rant about your personal problems

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Saying "I don't know" and encouraging them to post on the class forum

4. What is an accurate description of the "stress olympics"?

    A competition every semester where TAs vote on the most stressed out student to win a prize.
    A competition every semester where TAs vote on the most stressed out student to give an automatic TA offer for next semester.
    A situation where a student expresses being stressed out and a TA responds by saying that many peers are more stressed out.
    A situation where a student expresses being stressed out and a TA responds by saying they're even more stressed out.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: A situation where a student expresses being stressed out and a TA responds by saying they're even more stressed out.

5. What "saying" best describes the philosophy of debugging during OH?

    Teach a man to fish.
    An apple a day keeps the doctor away.
    Computers are stupid, but they're stupid quickly.
    That's the last straw.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Teach a man to fish.
