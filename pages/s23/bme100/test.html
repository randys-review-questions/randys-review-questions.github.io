BME 100: Design of a Medical Instrument

Based on content taught by Srivalleesha Mallidi in Spring 2023.

1. All of the following major medical innovations are medical devices EXCEPT...

    X-rays
    Cochlear implants
    Pulse oximeters
    Microarrays

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Microarrays

2. Platinum wire RTDs (the de-facto industry standard temperature sensor) have different _____ at different temperatures and have _____ temperature coefficients.

    resistances, positive
    resistances, negative
    voltages, positive
    voltages, negative

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: resistances, positive

3. Suppose that in a voltage divider circuit containing two resistors, resistor \(A\) has a fixed resistance of \(100\) \(\Omega\) and resistor \(B\) has a variable resistance. In order to maximize sensitivity (change in voltage across \(B\) with respect to change in \(B\)'s resistance), resistor \(B\) should have approximately what resistance?

    \(0\) \(\Omega\)
    \(50\) \(\Omega\)
    \(100\) \(\Omega\)
    \(\infty\) \(\Omega\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(100\) \(\Omega\)

4. Consider the following image of a Wheatstone Bridge circuit. In order for the bridge to be balanced, what must be the resistance of the resistor marked with an \(x\)?

    \(75\) \(\Omega\)
    \(150\) \(\Omega\)
    \(200\) \(\Omega\)
    \(300\) \(\Omega\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(75\) \(\Omega\)

5. The derivative of a ramp signal with respect to time is...

    an impulse signal.
    a step signal.
    a parabolic signal.
    another ramp signal.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: a step signal.

6. Strain gauges are devices that can be used as sensors, as the amount of strain affects the resistance of the strain gauge. Specifically, strain significantly changes what property of the fibers within the device to increase resistance?

    Increases the resistivity
    Increases the dielectric constant
    Increases the length
    Decreases the cross-sectional area

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Increases the length

7. Suppose that we have a series RC circuit containing some AC voltage source, and we are plotting the frequency of the voltage source on the x axis and the capacitive reactance (i.e. the magnitude of the impedance of the capacitor) on the y axis. This plot would have a _____ shape, and as the capacitance of the capacitor increases,...

    hyperbolic, the curve becomes sharper.
    hyperbolic, the curve becomes less sharp.
    linear, the slope increases.
    linear, the slope decreases.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: hyperbolic, the curve becomes sharper.

8. Suppose that you are designing a bandpass filter using a series RLC circuit. The output voltage should be the voltage across...

    the resistor.
    the capacitor.
    the inductor.
    both the capacitor and the inductor.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: the resistor.

9. What is the resonant frequency of a series RLC circuit where \(R = 4\) \(\Omega\), \(L = 8\) \(H\), and \(C = 2\) \(F\)?

    \(\frac{1}{16}\) rad/sec
    \(\frac{1}{8}\) rad/sec
    \(\frac{1}{4}\) rad/sec
    \(\frac{1}{2}\) rad/sec

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(\frac{1}{4}\) rad/sec

10. Defibrillators contain _____ circuits that produce the Lown waveform, which is _____.

    critically damped, monophasic
    critically damped, biphasic
    underdamped, monophasic
    underdamped, biphasic

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: underdamped, biphasic

11. Suppose that the inverting input voltage to an operational amplifier is \(3\) \(V\) and the noninverting input voltage is \(4\) \(V\). Further suppose that the operational amplifier has an open loop gain of \(10\) and that the positive and negative power supplies are \(+15\) \(V\) and \(-15\) \(V\), respectively. Assuming that there is no feedback, what is the output voltage?

    \(10\) \(V\)
    \(11\) \(V\)
    \(15\) \(V\)
    \(70\) \(V\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(10\) \(V\)

12. Which of the following operational amplifier circuits is a voltage follower?

    a
    b
    c
    d

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: b

13. All of the following are true statements regarding active filters EXCEPT...

    Active filters generally require more power than passive filters.
    Active filters can provide a gain of greater than 1, whereas passive filters cannot provide a gain of more than 1.
    Both active and passive filters are designed to "pass" certain frequencies and attenuate others.
    An active lowpass filter can be created by taking an inverting amplifier circuit and connecting a capacitor in series with the feedback resistor.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: An active lowpass filter can be created by taking an inverting amplifier circuit and connecting a capacitor in series with the feedback resistor.

14. Which picture most accurately represents how the pins of a breadboard are implicitly connected (before adding any wires)?

    a
    b
    c
    d

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: b

15. Suppose that a signal is represented as follows in the frequency domain. Assuming that all components of the signal are of interest, what (in practice) should be the minimum sampling frequency of a digitizer of this signal?

    \(100\) \(Hz\)
    \(200\) \(Hz\)
    \(400\) \(Hz\)
    \(1600\) \(Hz\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(1600\) \(Hz\)
