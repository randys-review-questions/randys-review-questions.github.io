Randy's Review Questions

AP Latin: Vergil and Caesar

Based on content taught by Kathryn Oparowski in the 2018-2019 school year.

1. In Book 1 of De Bello Gallico by Julius Caesar, why did Caesar not grant the Helvetii's request?

    Memory
    Fortune
    Distrust
    Threats
    Both the first option and the fourth option

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Memory

2. In Book 4 of De Bello Gallico, the attack on the Britons in 4.24 has many parallels with...

    the Battle of Saratoga
    the Attack on Fort Sumter
    the Caning of Charles Sumner
    D-Day
    the Attack on Hiroshima and Nagasaki

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: D-Day

3. Book 5 of De Bello Gallico is primarily concerned with which season(s)? Select all that apply.

    Spring
    Summer
    Fall
    Winter

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answers were: Winter

4. According to Book 6 of De Bello Gallico, what are the Druids exempt from?

    Conducting Sacrifices
    Managing Religion
    Military Service
    Voting for Druids
    None of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Military Service

5. In the first three words of The Aeneid by Vergil, "Arma virumque cano," what does the word "arma" represent?

    Book 1 of The Aeneid
    Books 2-3 of The Aeneid
    Books 4-6 of The Aeneid
    Books 7-12 of The Aeneid
    Book 13 of The Aeneid

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Books 7-12 of The Aeneid

6. In the fall of Troy scene in Book 2 of The Aeneid, when Aeneas was angry at Helen, who quelled his anger?

    Aeolus
    Neptune
    Juno
    Venus
    Turnus

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Venus

7. In Book 4 of The Aeneid, where did Dido and Aeneas get married?

    A cave
    A church
    A courthouse
    Mt. Olympus
    Caesar's Gaul

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: A cave

8. Who was the sailor in Book 6 of The Aeneid?

    Aeneas
    Prophetess
    Anchises
    Juno
    Charon

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Charon

9. Which of the following clauses use a subjunctive? Select two answers.

    Relative Clause
    Purpose Clause
    Conditional Clause

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answers were: Purpose Clause, Conditional Clause

10. When scanning in dactylic hexameter, what is present in the words "pietate hiems"?

    Diphthong
    x or "z"
    Elision
    Syncopated word
    None of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Elision
