Randy's Review Questions

AP Physics 1

Based on content taught by Julie Barker in the 2018-2019 school year.

1. Which of the following is/are (a) necessary step(s) for showing work in Ms. Barker's class? Select all that apply.

    List Givens
    List Find(s)
    Solve with Symbols
    Label Answers with Units

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answers were: List Givens, List Find(s), Solve with Symbols, Label Answers with Units

2. The average acceleration is the...

    Slope of the Position vs. Time Graph
    Area under the Position vs. Time Graph
    Slope of the Velocity vs. Time Graph
    Area under the Velocity vs. Time Graph
    Slope of the Acceleration vs. Time Graph

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Slope of the Velocity vs. Time Graph

3. The action-reaction pair of the force of weight on an object is...

    The normal force of the object on the surface
    The normal force of the surface on the object
    The \(x\) component of the weight
    The \(y\) component of the weight
    The gravitational force of the object on the Earth

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The gravitational force of the object on the Earth

4. The period of a pendulum is dependent on its...

    Mass
    Length
    Chemical Composition
    Both the first option and the second option
    None of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Length

5. Which of the following indicates a change in angular momentum? Select two answers.

    A force acting on the center of mass of the system
    A force acting at a distance from the center of mass of the system
    A change in angular velocity
    A constant angular velocity

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answers were: A force acting at a distance from the center of mass of the system, A change in angular velocity

6. According to the work energy theorem, net work is the...

    Change in potential energy
    Change in kinetic energy
    Change in electrical energy
    Change in conservative force
    Change in non conservative force

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Change in kinetic energy

7. A sound wave travels through air, then enters water. Which of the following remains constant through this transition?

    Wavelength
    Frequency
    Speed
    Only the first option and the second option
    All of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Frequency

8. The principle that when two waves interfere, the displacement at each point of a standing wave is the sum of the displacement of each individual wave is what phenomenon?

    Constructive Interference
    Destructive Interference
    Quantum Mechanical Model
    Superposition
    Snell's Law

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Superposition

9. Conventional current is...

    in the direction that protons travel
    in the direction opposite the direction that protons travel
    in the direction that positrons travel
    in the direction that electrons travel
    in the direction opposite the direction that electrons travel

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: in the direction opposite the direction that electrons travel

10. An anti-neutrino is the product of what decay process?

    Alpha decay
    Positive beta decay
    Negative beta decay
    Electron capture
    Gamma decay

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Negative beta decay
