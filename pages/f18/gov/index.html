Randy's Review Questions

AP US Government and Politics

Based on content taught by Matt Swanson in the 2018-2019 school year.

1. The idea of separation of powers originated from which Enlightenment thinker?

    Montesquieu
    Jean Jacques Rousseau
    Thomas Hobbes
    John Locke
    Matthew Swanson

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Montesquieu

2. Cooperative Federalism began largely during which time period?

    1776-1800
    1800-1900
    1900-1950
    1950-2019
    2019-2765

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 1900-1950

3. A poll designed to influence, rather than record, public opinion is which type of poll?

    Entrance Poll
    Exit Poll
    Tracking Poll
    Website Poll
    Push Poll

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Push Poll

4. Which type of primary is open only to party members?

    Caucus Primary
    Closed Primary
    Open Primary
    Blanket Primary
    Runoff Primary

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Closed Primary

5. Which of the following are interest group activities? Select two answers.

    Lobby to Congress
    Protest
    Rule on Constitutionality of Laws
    Pass Legislation

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answers were: Lobby to Congress, Protest

6. Who of the following is/are involved in the legislative process (for formal legislation)? Select all that apply.

    House Committee on Rules
    Congressional Subcomittees
    The President

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answers were: House Committee on Rules, Congressional Subcomittees, The President

7. One action, other than passing legislation, that Congress can take to limit the bureaucracy's power is...

    None of the above

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Holding hearings

    The US Supreme Court
