1. All of the following are contributing factors to environmental racism EXCEPT...

    More polluting facilities are present in non-White than White neighborhoods.
    The decision by the Trump administration to end much federal environmental justice work.
    People of color are exposed to a larger volume of pollutants than White people.
    All of the above are contributing factors to environmental racism.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: All of the above are contributing factors to environmental racism.

2. Which of the following statements about disparities regarding solar panel adoption is true?

    Communities of color are more likely to adopt solar panels than white communities, but there is no evidence of a racial disparity after adjusting for income disparities.
    Communities of color are more likely to adopt solar panels than white communities, and this racial disparity exists even after adjusting for income disparities.
    Communities of color are less likely to adopt solar panels than white communities, but there is no evidence of a racial disparity after adjusting for income disparities.
    Communities of color are less likely to adopt solar panels than white communities, and this racial disparity exists even after adjusting for income disparities.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Communities of color are less likely to adopt solar panels than white communities, and this racial disparity exists even after adjusting for income disparities.

3. Which of the following is a true statement regarding bias and machine learning models?

    Bias in machine learning models can result from disproportionate representation in the data used to train the model.
    As long as a machine learning model is trained on data that is representative of the population, it will not be biased.
    All of the above
    None of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Bias in machine learning models can result from disproportionate representation in the data used to train the model.

4. In Python syntax, what does the equals operator (=) do?

    Separates the left and right side of an equation
    Assigns a variable to some value
    Tests two variable names for equality
    Tests two values for equality

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Assigns a variable to some value
