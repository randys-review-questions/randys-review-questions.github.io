Randy's Review Questions

MUS 69: Gospel Choir

Based on content taught by David Coleman in Fall 2020.

You are currently in test mode. To switch to learn mode, click here.

1. The difference between Gospel music and the Blues is that Gospel music is _____, while the Blues is _____.

    sacred, secular
    secular, sacred
    classical, romantic
    romantic, classical

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: sacred, secular

2. Two primal instruments are...

    woodwinds and brass
    percussion and voice
    winds and strings
    winds and percussion

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: percussion and voice

3. Slavery is not just about forcing people to do work. It is about...

    forcing people to do extremely laborious work.
    actively damaging the health of enslaved people.
    power and dominance over a human being.
    forcing people to sing.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: power and dominance over a human being.

4. Field hollers ("work songs") often involve...

    strict time signatures.
    call and response.
    silence.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: call and response.

5. How was the Blues scale born?

    Notes commonly used in Gospel music were put together into a scale.
    Someone tried every combination of tri-tones until they found a sequence that sounded good.
    Notes were removed from the relatively universal pentatonic scale.
    People tried to sing the diatonic scale, but sung it incorrectly.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: People tried to sing the diatonic scale, but sung it incorrectly.

6. Enslaved people in the United States viewed which river as the "Jordan"?

    Mississippi River
    Connecticut River
    Ohio River
    Hudson River

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Ohio River

7. In Gospel music's early history, many churches considered it...

    sacred.
    traditional.
    innovative.
    unholy.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: unholy.

8. The invitation of members who do not share the black heritage of Gospel music but want to honor it has what effect on the choir?

    Increases the size of the choir
    Decreases the level of heritage of the choir
    Increases the power of the choir
    All of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: All of the above

9. Fill in the blank: "It is His will that every need be supplied. You are important to me. I need you to _____"

    win.
    be encouraged.
    survive.
    dance.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: survive.

10. Gospel music is closely tied with the gospels of the Bible, which are which four books?

    Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers
    Psalms, Proverbs, Song of Songs, Isaiah
    Matthew, Mark, Luke, John
    Acts, Romans, Jude, Revelation

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Matthew, Mark, Luke, John
