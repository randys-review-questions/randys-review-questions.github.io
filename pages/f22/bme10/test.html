1. What is the voltage between point \(A\) and point \(B\)?

    The amount of electrical charge that flows from \(A\) to \(B\) per unit time.
    The amount of electrical charge that flows from \(B\) to \(A\) per unit time.
    The amount of energy gained by a unit charge that moves from \(A\) to \(B\).
    The amount of energy gained by a unit charge that moves from \(B\) to \(A\).

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The amount of energy gained by a unit charge that moves from \(B\) to \(A\).

2. A current source (\(12\) \(A\)) supplies power to \(2\) resistors connected in parallel. \(R_1\) is \(1\) \(\Omega\) and \(R_2\) is \(3\) \(\Omega\). What is the current that flows through \(R_1\)?

    \(3\) \(A\)
    \(4\) \(A\)
    \(9\) \(A\)
    \(12\) \(A\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(9\) \(A\)

3. A Thevenin equivalent circuit represents a circuit as...

    a current source connected in series with a resistor.
    a current source connected in parallel with a resistor.
    a voltage source connected in series with a resistor.
    a voltage source connected in parallel with a resistor.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: a voltage source connected in series with a resistor.

4. A \(12\) \(V\) source supplies power to a \(3\) \(\Omega\) resistor connected in series with an inductor with an inductance of \(1\) \(H\). Under steady state conditions, what is the voltage difference across the resistor?

    \(0\) \(V\)
    \(4\) \(V\)
    \(9\) \(V\)
    \(12\) \(V\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(12\) \(V\)

5. The cell membrane is most similar to what circuit element?

    Resistors
    Capacitors
    Inductors
    Operational Amplifiers

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Capacitors

6. Suppose that \(x\) represented a quantity measured in \(m\). Let \(\delta\) represent the Dirac delta "function." \(\delta(x)\) would have what unit?

    \(m^{-1}\)
    \(m\)
    \(m^2\)
    (dimensionless)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(m^{-1}\)

7. In second order circuits (two energy storing elements), the amount of damping affects behavior. In the critically damped case (where damping coefficient (\(\alpha\)) equals undamped resonant frequency (\(\omega_0\))), the solutions of the characteristic equation (\(s_1\) and \(s_2\)) are...

    both real and positive.
    both zero.
    both real and negative.
    both nonreal.

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: both real and negative.

8. For an AC circuit, the current through a capacitor _____ the voltage difference across that capacitor by \(90^{\circ}\), and the capacitor's impedance can be calculated by _____.

(\(z_C\) = impedance, \(\omega\) = frequency, \(C\) = capacitance)

    lags, \(z_C = j \omega C\)
    lags, \(z_C = \frac{1}{j \omega C}\)
    leads, \(z_C = j \omega C\)
    leads, \(z_C = \frac{1}{j \omega C}\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: leads, \(z_C = \frac{1}{j \omega C}\)

9. The voltage across a circuit element is \(20\) \(V\) with a phase of \(60^{\circ}\), and a current of \(10\) \(A\) with a phase of \(30^{\circ}\) flows through that element. What is the impedance of the circuit element?

    \(2\) \(\Omega\) with a phase of \(2^{\circ}\)
    \(2\) \(\Omega\) with a phase of \(30^{\circ}\)
    \(200\) \(\Omega\) with a phase of \(0^{\circ}\)
    \(200\) \(\Omega\) with a phase of \(90^{\circ}\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(2\) \(\Omega\) with a phase of \(30^{\circ}\)

10. The power factor seen by a circuit element is \(0.5\) leading. What is the power angle (\(\theta_v - \theta_i\))?

    \(-60^{\circ}\)
    \(-30^{\circ}\)
    \(30^{\circ}\)
    \(60^{\circ}\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(-60^{\circ}\)

11. Suppose that for a given circuit, the Thevenin equivalent voltage is \(30\) \(V\) with a phase of \(0^{\circ}\), and the Thevenin impedance is \((3+j4)\) \(\Omega\). What load resistance (for a resistor connected to the terminals) will absorb the maximum average power?

    \(3\) \(\Omega\)
    \(5\) \(\Omega\)
    \((3+j4)\) \(\Omega\)
    \((3-j4)\) \(\Omega\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(5\) \(\Omega\)

12. The Fourier Series represents any _____ function as an infinite sum of sinusoidal functions.

    periodic
    polynomial
    continuous
    differentiable

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: periodic

13. Consider the circuit depicted below. This is an example of a(n) _____ filter, and has a transfer function magnitude of \(0\) \(dB\) at _____ frequencies.

    lowpass, low
    lowpass, high
    highpass, low
    highpass, high

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: highpass, high

14. What type of operational amplifier circuit has a voltage gain of \(1+\frac{R_2}{R_1}\)?

    Comparator
    Inverting Amplifier
    Non Inverting Amplifier
    Voltage Follower

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Non Inverting Amplifier

15. Which of the following circuits is an integrator amplifier?

    a
    b
    c
    d

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: a
