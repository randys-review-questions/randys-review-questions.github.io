1. The correct way to notate Point \(X\) would be...

    \(X\)
    Point \(X\)
    \(X.\)
    .

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(X\)

2. A line that crosses over two parallel lines is called a(n)...

    radical
    transversal
    coinciding line
    intruder

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: transversal

3. The distance formula is derived from...

    The volume formula for a sphere
    The pythagorean theorem
    The side splitter theorem
    \(\pi\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The pythagorean theorem

4. Which of the following postulates does not prove that a triangle is congruent?

    SAS
    SSS
    SSA
    ASA

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: SSA

5. The point of intersection between the three medians of a triangle is the...

    Centroid
    Incenter
    Circumcenter
    Orthocenter

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Centroid

6. A 30-60-90 triangle would have which of the following reference lengths?

    \(x\), \(2x\), and \(x \sqrt{2}\)
    \(x\), \(x\), and \(x \sqrt{2}\)
    \(x\), \(2x\), and \(x \sqrt{3}\)
    \(x^2\), \(2 x^2\), and \(x \sqrt{7}\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(x\), \(2x\), and \(x \sqrt{3}\)

7. \(\sin(x) = \frac{a}{b}\). What is \(a\)?

    The hypotenuse
    The leg opposite to angle \(x\)
    The leg adjacent to angle \(x\)
    The arm adjacent to angle \(x\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The leg opposite to angle \(x\)

8. The diagonals of a rhombus are...

    Congruent
    Similar
    Perpendicular
    Parallel

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Perpendicular

9. Based on the formula for the area of a regular polygon, to find the area, you need the perimeter and the...

    Radius
    Apothem
    Side Length
    Number of sides

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Apothem

10. The volume of a sphere is calculated by which expression?

    \(\pi r^2 h\)
    \(4 \pi r^2\)
    \(\frac{4}{3} \pi r^3\)
    \(\pi r^3\)

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: \(\frac{4}{3} \pi r^3\)
