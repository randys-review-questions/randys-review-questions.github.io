1. During the tuning process, instruments in a band are tuned to the...

    Oboe
    Trumpet
    Tuba
    Clarinet

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Clarinet

2. What is the primary part the audience is listening to in a band?

    Melody
    Countermelody
    Harmony
    Bass Line

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Melody

3. When you see this accidental (#), you should be playing the note _____ the note indicated.

    a half step above
    a half step below
    a whole step above
    a whole step below

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: a half step above

4. F#=Gb is an example of a(n)...

    Interval
    Octave
    Enharmonic
    Expressive marking

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Enharmonic

5. Tempo is referred to as...

    Speed of the piece of music
    Style of the piece of music
    Notes on the music
    Volume of the piece of music

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Speed of the piece of music

6. In order to ensure note accuracy, faster rhythms should be practiced at a _____ at first.

    Smoother Style
    Slower Tempo
    Louder Dynamic
    Different Key

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Slower Tempo

7. How many trumpet parts are there in a song?

    2
    3
    4
    Depends

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Depends

8. Note accuracy is...

    How loud or soft a piece of music is
    How fast or slow a piece of music is
    How correctly notes are played
    How the style of the notes are carried out

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: How correctly notes are played

9. In the 2016-2017 school year, what is the report time before a concert in CONCERT band?

    5:45pm
    6:15pm
    6:30pm
    6:45pm

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 6:30pm

10. In the 2016-2017 school year, what day of the week are band concerts on?

    Tuesday
    Thursday
    Friday
    Saturday

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Thursday
