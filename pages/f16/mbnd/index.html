Randy's Review Questions

Marching Band

Based on content taught by the CHSMRB instructors in Fall 2016.

1. During any type of marching band performance, all horns should be angled toward...

    The sideline
    The drum major
    The box
    The ground

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The box

2. According to instructor Evan, marching correctly is ____.

    Comfortable
    Uncomfortable
    Easy
    Hard

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Uncomfortable

3. In Marching Band, a "step" on a coordinate sheet refers to...

    An 8 to 5
    A 16 to 5
    A 5 to 8
    A 20 to 79

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: An 8 to 5

4. In the 2016-2017 school year, what time are evening rehearsals in marching band on Tuesdays and Thursdays?

    4:00pm-7:00pm
    5:30pm-8:30pm
    6:15pm-9:15pm
    7:30pm-10:30pm

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 5:30pm-8:30pm

5. In a 90 degree slide (marching), the hip should be at a __ degree angle.

    30
    45
    60
    90

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 45

6. Besides Saturday shows, what other events does the Marching Band perform at?

    Volleyball games
    Football games
    Math league competitions
    Cocoa and Cram

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Football games

7. On average (roughly), around what time do marching band REHEARSALS end on Saturdays?

    10:01am
    4:00pm
    11:00pm
    3:30am

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 4:00pm

8. When marching backwards, the ____ of your foot should NEVER touch the ground.

    Platform
    Toes
    Heel
    Ball

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Heel

9. Which exercise in marching band is designed to specifically focus on basic marching technique?

    Slow Roll
    Traffic
    Box
    Flow

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Slow Roll

10. What is the title of the 2016 CHSMRB Show?

    New World
    Out of the Darkness and Into the Light
    Duality
    The Midas Touch

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The Midas Touch
