Randy's Review Questions

Physical Education

Based on content taught by Sarah Mik in the 2019-2020 school year.

1. Which of the following gyms is in the basement near the auditorium?

    West Gym
    East Gym
    North Gym
    South Gym

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: West Gym

2. All of the following are true about tennis EXCEPT...

    The same server serves for the whole game
    The scoring in the game goes: 15, 30, 45, then game
    From a deuce, a team needs to score twice in a row in order to win the game
    A serve should land in the diagonally opposite service box

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The scoring in the game goes: 15, 30, 45, then game

3. A score of one below par in golf is called a(n)...

    Bogey
    Double Bogey
    Birdie
    Eagle

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Birdie

4. In flag football, who is the one who remains behind the line of scrimmage after "hike" is called and throws/passes the ball?

    Center
    Quarterback
    Receiver
    Defender

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Quarterback

5. In Archery, hitting the dead center of the target is worth 10 points. You have six arrows, and you use all of them in each round. What is the maximum possible score for each round?

    6
    10
    16
    60

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 60

6. Which of the following would be a violation of the rules of badminton?

    Hitting the shuttlecock into the net
    Hitting the shuttlecock over the net
    Using a backhand serve
    Walking underneath the net to the other side mid-game

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Walking underneath the net to the other side mid-game

7. Rally scoring in volleyball means that...

    Only the server's team may score
    Only the receiving team may score
    No one is ever allowed to score
    Someone scores every round

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Someone scores every round

8. During a volleyball "spike," one should be particularly cautious about...

    Touching the net
    Touching the ceiling
    Accidentally ingesting the volleyball
    Creating holes in the gym floor

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Touching the net

9. An individual's maximum heart rate is one's age subtracted from what number?

    160
    200
    220
    250

Correct!

Incorrect. Try again.

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 220

10. All of the following are types of aerobic exercise EXCEPT...

    Running
    Swimming

    Biking

Correct!
