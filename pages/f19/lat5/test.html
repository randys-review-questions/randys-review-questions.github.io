1. The fourth principal part of a verb is the verb's...

    Present Active Participle
    Future Active Participle
    Perfect Passive Participle
    Future Passive Participle

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Perfect Passive Participle

2. In the present active subjunctive form of a second conjugation verb, the vowel ("e") as part of the infinitive stem is changed to a(n)...

    ea
    a
    ia
    iu

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: ea

3. What is the ending of a neuter singular noun that is in the second declension, nominative case?

    us
    um
    i
    a

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: um

4. After the Jews in Masada recognized the inevitability of the Roman invasion, they...

    fortified their camp and made their preparations to fight
    fled to another site
    dug deep tunnels and hid underground
    arranged for a mass suicide

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: arranged for a mass suicide

5. The phrase "per dicendum disce" contains what grammatical structure?

    Gerund
    Gerundive
    Supine
    Relative Pronoun

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Gerund

6. In Pliny 10.96, Pliny writes a letter to Emperor Trajan asking him for advice about what?

    How to more effectively run the Colosseum
    How to dispose of the dolphin's corpse
    How to handle Christians
    How to find a date in Rome

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: How to handle Christians

7. Although Cleopatra had an affair with Julius Caesar, she married...

    Augustus Caesar
    Caesarion
    Ptolemy I
    Mark Antony

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Mark Antony

8. Which of the following is the last letter of the Greek alphabet?

    Beta
    Omega
    Theta
    Zeta

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Omega

9. According to Mary Beard, in ancient Rome, when were children first put to work?

    At the moment they were born
    Once they took their first steps
    As soon as they were fit and able
    When they reached adulthood

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: As soon as they were fit and able

10. On a tombstone inscription, "Imp." is often short for..

    Imperator
    Imperative
    Imperavit
    Vixit Annos

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Imperator
