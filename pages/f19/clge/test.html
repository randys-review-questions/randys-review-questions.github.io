Randy's Review Questions

College Admissions

Based on my experience applying to colleges from Cheshire High School in the 2019-2020 school year.

You are currently in test mode. To switch to learn mode, click here.

1. What is the maximum number of colleges that one can apply to via the Common Application?

    10
    15
    20
    There is no limit

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 20

2. All of the following schools use the Common Application EXCEPT...

    Tufts University
    California Institute of Technology
    Yale University
    Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Massachusetts Institute of Technology

3. The National College Decision Day, when people typically have to commit to a certain school, is on what date?

    April 1st
    April 15th
    May 1st
    June 1st

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: May 1st

4. People typically hear back from Early Decision I /Early Action schools around...

    the end of November
    mid-December
    the beginning of February
    the end of March

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: mid-December

5. In Cheshire High School, students typically request letters of recommendation through...

    Turning in a yellow form (at least it was yellow in 2019)
    Sending a request through Naviance
    All of the above
    None of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: All of the above

6. Which of the following is MOST important in a college application?

    Having a high GPA and a good SAT/ACT score
    Having a large amount of extracurricular activities
    Writing thoughtful and polished essays
    Submitting the application by the deadline

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Submitting the application by the deadline

7. Some schools have a system where you hear back a certain amount of time after submitting your application, potentially before others have even applied. This is known as...

    Early Decision
    Early Action
    Rolling Admissions
    Regular Decision

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Rolling Admissions

8. What is the difference between Early Decision and Early Action?

    Early Decision is a binding agreement to attend if accepted; Early Action is not
    Early Decision involves guaranteed admittance; Early Action does not
    Early Decision involves hearing back before the regular decision deadline; Early Action does not
    There is no difference between Early Decision and Early Action

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Early Decision is a binding agreement to attend if accepted; Early Action is not

9. On most applications, Cheshire High School CCP (Competitive College Prep) classes (not including Advanced Chem or Math Analysis) would be classified as...

    College Level Courses
    Accelerated Courses
    Honors Level Courses
    Useless Courses

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Honors Level Courses

10. What is the word limit for the Common Application Personal Essay (at least in 2019)?

    250
    400
    500
    650

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 650
