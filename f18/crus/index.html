Randy's Review Questions

Concert Choir

Based on content taught by Beth Malvezzi in the 2018-2019 school year.

1. All of the following are forms of good posture EXCEPT...

    Eyes looking at the audience
    Shoulders down and back
    Feet flat on the floor
    Arms on the side

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Eyes looking at the audience

2. What term refers to each part sounding unified as one voice?

    Interpretation
    Phrasing
    Balance
    Blend

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Blend

3. A sfortzando is basically the same as...

    An accent
    A marcato
    A crescendo
    A forte-piano followed by a crescendo

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: A forte-piano followed by a crescendo

4. When inhaling before singing, where should air be targeted towards?

    The top of the lungs
    The bottom of the lungs
    The left lung
    The right lung

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: The bottom of the lungs

5. In terms of audience etiquitte, what is one major difference between sports games and concerts?

    It is appropriate in sports games to talk, but not during concerts
    It is appropriate to remove a crying child during sports games, but not during concerts
    It is appropriate in concerts to talk, but not during sports games
    It is appropriate to remove a crying child during concerts, but not during sports games

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: It is appropriate in sports games to talk, but not during concerts

6. In the 2018-2019 school year, how many minutes before a chorus concert is report time?

    15
    30
    45
    60

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 45

7. What type of exercise is not advised for singers?

    Running
    Swimming
    Heavy weight lifting
    Yoga

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Heavy weight lifting

8. Why is diction important when singing?

    So the audience can get on their phones ASAP
    So the audience can understand what the singers are saying
    So the audience can be driven out of the auditorium
    So the audience can chase vampires away

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: So the audience can understand what the singers are saying

9. Which of the following was a Broadway medley sung in the 2019 Choral Broadway Concert?

    The Lion King
    My Fair Lady
    Jekyll and Hyde
    Phantom of the Opera

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Jekyll and Hyde

10. In a barbershop quartet, which part usually has the melody?

    Highest part
    Second highest part
    Second lowest part
    Lowest part

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Second highest part
