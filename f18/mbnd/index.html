Randy's Review Questions

Marching Band

Based on content taught by the CHSMRB instructors in Fall 2018.

1. Which of the following describes proper marching technique?

    Lifting up your foot, then plopping it back down
    Pushing off the platform of the stationary foot
    Jumping and landing
    Falling Down

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Pushing off the platform of the stationary foot

2. From the audience's point of view, side 1 is ______ side 2.

    In front of
    Behind
    Left of
    Right of

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Left of

3. Center X is...

    On the 50 yd line
    On the front hash
    On the back hash
    Two of the above

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: On the 50 yd line

4. How many counts are there in the entirety of the "You've Changed" show?

    429
    856
    1361
    2945

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: 1361

5. Which of the following was not incorporated in the 2019 CHSMRB show?

    Mahler
    Jar of Hearts
    Changes
    You've Changed

Correct!

Incorrect. The correct answer was: Jar of Hearts
