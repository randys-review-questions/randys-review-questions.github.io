Randy's Review Questions

Welcome to a website containing multiple choice review questions that I have written for all classes that I have taken at Tufts University and Cheshire High School. Click the appropriate link below to reach the page containing the desired practice quiz.

For university courses, I write 5 questions for every credit that the class is worth. For example, I would write 15 questions for a 3 SHU course. For high school courses, I generally write 10 questions per class, except there are certain classes that don't meet that much where I only write 5.

When taking these quizzes, you have the option to check answers question by question or wait until the end to check all answers. Correct answers will not be revealed if you opt for the former (allowing for more attempts on that question), but will be revealed if you opt for the latter.

The formatting of these quizzes was heavily inspired by this quiz on the CS 40 webpage on Mark Sheldon's Whirlwind Tour of C.

NOTE: Although I do my best to ensure that the quiz questions and corresponding answers are accurate, I cannot guarantee that I didn't screw up. If you find any problems, please raise an issue here .
